Introduction

MultiNet EUR 2021.12.000 contains the highlights and improvements listed below, compared to MultiNet EUR 2021.09.000 and apart from all the normal database updates.
Note that we only list the countries subject to changes.

General

  • Removal of Route Number Information Colors which was already deprecated since the specification update of MultiNet 2020.06.000 release.

  • Road geometry was updated in line with reality.
